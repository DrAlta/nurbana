== 1 ========= Product Description ==========
Nurbana is freeware 3D NURBS modeling tool developed by Nurbana, Inc.
incorporated under the state of Delaware.  Nurbana is not opensource
and that will remain until Nurbana, Inc. decides otherwise.  Nurbana
may be used for any purpose and may NOT be distributed commercialy.

Our website is located at www.nurbana.cx.  At the website you will
find an online manual describing how to use Nurbana we well as other
resources.

Thank you for choosing Nurbana.

== 2 ======== Supported Platforms ==========
Nurbana is currently supported on 6 platforms:
- BeOS (x86)
- FreeBSD (x86)
- IRIX (MIPS) 32/64-bit
- Linux (x86)
- SunOS (Sparc)
- Windows (x86)

For installation instructions and further information, please
consult the website at: http://www.nurbana.cx
